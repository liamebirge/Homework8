  3. List the top 5 Victoria developers by accepted answer percentage.

    • Round(Sum(Case q.AcceptedAnswerId when a.Id then 1 else 0 end) * 100.0 / Count(a.Id), 1) as [Percentage Accepted]
    • from
    • Users,
    • Posts as a
    • join
    • Posts as q
    • on a.ParentId = q.Id
    • where
    • q.PostTypeId = 1
    • and a.PostTypeId = 2
    • and Users.Location like 'Victoria BC'
    • group by
    • a.OwnerUserId
    • having
    • Count(a.Id) between 0
    • and 100

  4. List the top 10 Victoria developers by accepted answer percentage on questions with the tag javascript.
    SELECT TOP 5 Tags.TagName, a.OwnerUserId AS [User Link], COUNT(a.Id) AS [Total Answers], Round(Sum(CASE q.AcceptedAnswerId WHEN a.Id THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) * 100.0 / Count(a.Id), 1) AS [Percentage Accepted] FROM Tags, Users, Posts AS a JOIN Posts AS q ON a.ParentId = q.Id WHERE q.PostTypeId = 1 AND a.PostTypeId = 2 and Users.Location like 'Victoria B.C.' and Tags.TagName like 'javascript' GROUP BY a.OwnerUserId, Tags.TagName HAVING Count(a.Id) BETWEEN 0 AND 100 ORDER BY [Percentage Accepted] Desc
